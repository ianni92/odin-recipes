Brazilian Beans

A hearty and flavorful dish made with black beans, garlic, and spices, a staple in Brazilian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups black beans (soaked overnight)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Drain and rinse the soaked beans.
  2. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the beans, bay leaves, and enough water to cover the beans by about 2 inches.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beans are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove bay leaves before serving.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro if desired. Serve with rice and enjoy!
