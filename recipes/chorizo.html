Brazilian Chorizo

A spicy, flavorful sausage that's a staple in Brazilian barbecues, perfect for grilling or frying.

Ingredients:

  • 6 Brazilian chorizo sausages
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced (optional)
  • Fresh lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sausages and cook until browned on all sides, about 10-12 minutes.
  2. Add the onions (and bell peppers if using) to the skillet and cook until softened and slightly caramelized.
  3. Serve the chorizo hot with a squeeze of fresh lime juice, alongside rice, beans, or crusty bread.
