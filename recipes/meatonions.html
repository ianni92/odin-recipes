Meat with Onions

A savory dish featuring tender beef slices cooked with caramelized onions, perfect with rice and beans.

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef (thinly sliced)
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until caramelized, about 10 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add more oil if needed, and cook the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the beef slices and cook until browned, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  4. Return the onions to the skillet, mix well, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Serve hot with rice and beans.
