Brigadeiro

Brigadeiro is a beloved Brazilian chocolate truffle, perfect for any celebration or as a sweet treat!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles for coating
  • Butter for greasing hands

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
  2. Stir constantly until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the sides of the pan (about 10 minutes).
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  4. Grease your hands with butter, scoop small portions of the mixture, and roll into balls.
  5. Roll each ball in chocolate sprinkles to coat.
  6. Place the brigadeiros in paper liners and refrigerate until firm. Enjoy!
